About Us

For all pests, including wood destruction insects, our exterminators in Maryland concentrate on residential , commercial and industrial pest management. We service all areas of Maryland and tackle every termite infestation with over 20 years of experience. We are members of the Pest Control Association of Maryland and the National Association for the Management of Pests.

Why Choose Us?

We’re a full-service company that is local, family owned and operated. In many respects, we’re not your typical pest control company. We do not run in and out of your house, for instance. Each day, we don’t have 20 appointments. We’ve got 6-8 at most. Speed is not what we are about. We are about providing unmatched service and results.

Our service technicians will thoroughly inspect the infestation. As well as the structural conditions inside, they look at the changing environmental conditions around the outside perimeter of your home and this is time consuming.

Our service technicians have also studied and graduated from the Entomology Programs and each of them in several states has earned their own license. They have studied the biology of the targeted pest and the chemistry of the materials used are all understood. The techs are also trained with the equipment we use in order to efficiently remove the pests while keeping the residents safe.
